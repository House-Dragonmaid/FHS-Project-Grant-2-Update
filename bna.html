BNA: Brand New Animal




BNA: Brand New Anima is a 2020 original anime series produced by Studio Trigger and premired on Netflix.
It got only one season with 12 episodes in it.

More info

BNA is about Michiru Kagemori, a person who escapes to Anima City after being transformed into a beastman. The show follows her and a wolf beastman named Shirou Ogami as they investigate Michiru's transformation and maintain peace in Anima City.
